# Features & Services Guide for Our Luxury Venue
We offer an exceptional experience featuring an unconventional, modern design that perfectly blends the luxury of indoor banqueting with the enchanting beauty of the outdoors.

### 1. Capacity & General Specifications
* **Total Capacity:** The venue comfortably accommodates up to **1,000 guests** (500 in the indoor hall + 500 in the outdoor garden).
* **Indoor Hall:** A panoramic glass hall with dual views of the lush garden and the elegant swimming pool.
* **Luxurious Ceiling:** A dynamic decorative ceiling adorned with floral arrangements, crystals, and premium chandeliers.
* **Prayer Room:** A quiet, fully equipped prayer room for the comfort and convenience of your guests.
* **Accessibility for All:** The venue's design prioritizes the comfort of **elderly guests and individuals with disabilities**, featuring smooth, safe pathways and ramps to ensure effortless access to all indoor and outdoor facilities.

### 2. Outdoor Spaces & Water Features
* **Lush Garden:** Equipped with elegant and comfortable outdoor seating.
* **Pool & Fountain:** An elegant overflow swimming pool and a dancing fountain that create a magical atmosphere.
* **Outdoor Bar:** A dedicated station that can be customized to serve mocktails/mojitos, desserts, or act as a coffee and dates corner, depending on your preference.

### 3. Special Bridal Touches (A Fairytale Entrance)
* **Bridal Walkway (Glass Bridge):** A stunning glass bridge extending over the pool, featuring an illuminated waterfall beneath it. Specifically designed for the bride's grand entrance (Zaffa) and serves as a breathtaking photo spot for the couple.
* **Comprehensive Bridal Suites:** Fully equipped private rooms including a bridal studio, a room for the bridesmaids/attendants, a master bedroom, and a sauna.

### 4. Complimentary Amenities & Equipment (Included in the Price)
Our venue stands out by offering **all the following amenities entirely free of charge**, with no hidden or additional fees:
* **Furniture & Décor:** 
  * Premium clear acrylic chairs and elegant golden chairs.
  * The option to furnish the entire hall with luxurious sofa seating.
  * Tables fully dressed with luxury linens, candelabras, and elegant vases.
  * A luxurious, dedicated table for the wedding cake.
* **Systems & Audio/Visuals:** 
  * Central air conditioning and heating, plus outdoor patio heaters for the garden.
  * A comprehensive, integrated sound system (indoor and outdoor).
  * Dynamic, ceiling-mounted disco lighting for the DJ session.
  * A central smoke extraction and ventilation system.
* **Kitchen Facilities:** A fully equipped kitchen ready for large banquets (including large cooking pots, kettles, serving dishes, chafing dishes, tongs, and stoves).

### 5. Wedding Stage Details & Interior Design Services
Upon signing the contract, we provide a **complimentary** consultation with a specialized interior designer to determine:
* The Wedding Stage (bridal seating/stage) design and color palette.
* The layout and arrangement of tables, chairs, and sofas tailored to your taste and guest count.

**Available Wedding Stage Options:**
1. **Complimentary Wedding Stage:** A sophisticated selection included in the base price.
2. **VIP Wedding Stage:** Available for a nominal additional fee (ranging from $200 to $300).
3. **Custom Wedding Stage:** Made-to-order designs priced according to your specific requirements.
